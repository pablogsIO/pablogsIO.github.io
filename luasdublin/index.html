Luas Dublin

Enjoy the beautiful city of Dublin with this amazing app. Luas Dublin will show you Luas tram stops and real-time information for each stop. In addition, it will calculate the route to your stop on foot and bike

Features

  • Shows Red and Green lines
  • Real-time information for each stop
  • Calculate the route to your stop by foot and bike
  • Beautiful design

Download

Luas Dublin is available for free in the App Store. It requires internet access and contains no advertising or in-app purchases
