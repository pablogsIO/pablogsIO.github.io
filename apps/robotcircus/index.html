Robot Circus

Your mission, if you decide to accept it, is to reach the left platform and get back to where you started. Use the robots to avoid falling into deep space and try not to collide with them. The robots have a pressure sensor, which if pressed will cause them to immediately self-destruct.

Good luck!!!
