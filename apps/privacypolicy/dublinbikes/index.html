Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. For that reason, Dublin Bikes doesn't collect your data or try and gain information about you via analytics.

Dublin Bikes use your current location (if you allow it) to show you the closest bike stations. We will not share your current location with other users or partners.

Dublin Bikes contains no advertising, analytics, or in-app purchases.
